old is the new awesome

Hey-lo there,
Katerina here.
In a way, I am a minimalist. I basically
own two things only:
an empty wallet and a head full of ideas.
And that’s all I need really.
At this very moment, over 98% of my wardrobe
is second-handed (third-handed usually hahah).
And I want nothing but to inspire you to do the same.
To show you it is very much possible and affordable.
And to prove that old stuff are awesome and deserve
a second chance.

naked

There was one moment in the fashion industry that
made me want to be part of it.
It was during Prague Fashion Week in 2019,
when a Czech fashion designer Iva Burketová
sent naked models on stage to start a debate about
sustainability and the fake world of fashion.
I figured out that to fix a problem, one cannot
only stand, one has to act.
Therefore, in the future, I am aiming to create
a fashion brand that will be 100% sustainable
Because I truly believe that there is no need
to produce any more clothing,
the only way to go is by reusing the tons
of materials that already exist.



Creativity and imagination
must become the new
needle and thread!


Have a pleasant time reading through♥
Love, Katerina
